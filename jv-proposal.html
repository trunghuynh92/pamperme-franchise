Which Model Suits You?

Choose JV If:
• You have 2.2B VND capital (not 4.5B)
• You prioritize ROI % over absolute returns
• You want faster payback (~16-18 months)
• You value PPM's ongoing strategic partnership
• You're comfortable with 67% ownership
Choose Standalone If:
• You have 4.5B+ VND capital available
• You want 100% ownership and control
• You prioritize absolute profit over ROI %
• You prefer no long-term equity partner
• You want flexibility in expansion timeline
