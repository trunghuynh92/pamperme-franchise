Training & Certification

Staff Development & Compliance Requirements

Training Structure

Training Type Trained By Who Gets Trained Fee
Initial - Manager PPM Store Manager Included
Initial - Caretakers PPM First batch of caretakers Included
Replacement - Caretakers Store Manager New caretakers No fee
Replacement - Manager PPM New/promoted managers 6M VND*

* Travel and accommodation costs not included

Compliance Requirements

Requirement Details
Minimum Certified Staff Each store must have: 1 certified manager + at least 1 certified caretaker at all times
Grace Period (New Hires) 3 months after becoming official employee to complete First-Aid certification
Certificate Renewal Every 12 months - staff must recertify before expiration
Training Schedule PPM hosts certification sessions quarterly (4 times per year)
Manager Certification All new managers (including internal promotions) must complete PPM training + pass exam

Estimated Annual Training Costs

Item Calculation Cost
Annual Certificate Renewal 6 staff × 2.4M × 1 certificate ~14.4M VND
New Hire Certifications ~2 new hires/year × 2.4M × 1 cert ~4.8M VND
Manager Replacement (if any) 0-1 per year 0-6M VND
Total Annual Training Budget ~19-25M VND
Note: These costs should be factored into the store's annual operating budget. Maintaining valid certifications is mandatory for continued franchise operation.
